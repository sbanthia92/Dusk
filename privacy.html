Dusk – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Dusk is a Chrome extension that applies a dark mode style to Amazon. This policy describes what data the extension does and does not collect.

Dusk collects no personal data of any kind. Nothing you do while using this extension is ever transmitted to any external server.

What We Collect

Nothing. Dusk does not collect, store remotely, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, search queries, purchase data, or usage analytics.

How Data Is Used

It isn't. The only data the extension touches is a single boolean preference (dark mode on or off) per domain. This preference is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.sync and is never read by or sent to any server operated by us or any third party.

Third Parties

Dusk does not integrate with, send data to, or load resources from any third-party service. There are no analytics libraries, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking pixels of any kind included in this extension.

Chrome Sync Note

If you have Chrome Sync enabled in your browser, Google may sync your chrome.storage.sync data across your devices as part of their standard sync infrastructure. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not ours. The data synced contains only your per-domain on/off toggle preference — nothing else.
